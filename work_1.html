The Energy Management System(EMS)

The Energy Management System (EMS) collects energy measurement data from the field, analyzes power demand and provides it to the user through graphs and charts. It allows users to monitor and schedule remotely to achieve energy management, power saving and money saving goals.

The product includes three webs service and one app for different users:

  1. Cloud web for field owner
  2. Edge web for field administrator
  3. Deploy web for field leader
  4. Deploy app for field staff

Process

Discovery & Ideate

We know what basic functions are needed for IoT products based on previous projects' experience, and then we use competitive analysis to understand the current industry status and trends. Because the majority of the competitors are B2B, it is almost Impossible to purchase them for use. I explored the product in three ways: the first was to register directly to use the free version, the second was to read the user guide of the product if registration was not possible, and the third way was to join the product's community to get a firsthand look at current user complaints and screenshots.
